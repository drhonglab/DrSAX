Who is Dr.Hong?


Euy-Shick Hong is a saxophonist who mainly plays jazz
and blues music and has concertized from Canada to Korea,
including the opening ceremonies of in 2002 World Cup Soccer
and Asian Games. He studied jazz at the Delta Community
Music School in Vancouver, and get a Master of music degree
in graduate school at Dongguk University.


Euy-Shick was studying various interactive works through
fascinated computer tech that physical computing, web-audio/visual programming,
realtime networking system for player.

fortunately, everyday he is trying and studying
for Ph.D course with saxophone,web-programming and
physical computing with arduino.
